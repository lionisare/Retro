JENNIE KIM

Jennie Kim, known mononymously as Jennie, is a South Korean singer, rapper, and actress. He was born on January 16, 1996 in Cheongdam-dong, Seoul, South Korea. Jennie spent five years studying in New Zealand before returning to South Korea in 2010.

Introduction

She debuted with her group, Blackpink, as main rapper and lead vocalist, which was founded by YG Entertainment in August 2016. In November 2018, Jennie debuted as a solo singer with the single entitled "Solo". This single was successful, and entered the ranks of the Gaon Digital Chart and Billboard World Digital Songs. In 2023, she made her first acting debut as Dyanne in the series The Idol, with the stage name Jennie Ruby Jane.
